Alex Luque Garcia — Visual Artist & CG Generalist

VFX Supervision, CG Lead and Creative Work

Alex Luque Garcia is a visual artist and CG generalist based in Barcelona, Spain. Specializing in VFX supervision and CG lead work for music videos, fashion films, commercials, and art direction projects.

Selected projects include Gentle Monster's BOLD 2025 campaign starring Tilda Swinton (directed by Mau Morgó, with music by Amnesia Scanner), CG work for Black MIDI's Welcome To Hell music video, No es la mía for Paul Thin, and ongoing collaborations with studios like Olimpic and Groove.

For commercial, music video, or creative inquiries, contact info@alexluquegarcia.com or follow @alexluquegarcia on Instagram.
